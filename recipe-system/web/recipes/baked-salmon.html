Baked Salmon with Lemon and Herbs

A elegant and healthy baked salmon fillet topped with fresh lemon juice, dill, and garlic. This restaurant-quality dish is simple to prepare and pairs beautifully with roasted vegetables.

Ingredients

Preparation Steps

1
Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
2
Place salmon fillets skin-side down on the prepared baking sheet.
3
Drizzle salmon with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
4
Distribute minced garlic, fresh dill, and thyme evenly over each fillet.
5
Top each salmon fillet with a few lemon slices.
6
Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes until salmon is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork.
7
Serve hot with additional fresh lemon wedges on the side.
