Mediterranean Greek Salad

A fresh and vibrant Greek salad with crisp vegetables, creamy feta cheese, Kalamata olives, and tangy lemon vinaigrette. Light, healthy, and perfect for warm summer days.

Ingredients

Preparation Steps

1
In a large bowl, combine cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red bell pepper, and red onion.
2
Add Kalamata olives and crumbled feta cheese to the vegetables.
3
In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, red wine vinegar, lemon juice, dried oregano, salt, and black pepper.
4
Pour dressing over salad and toss gently to combine.
5
Top with fresh chopped parsley.
6
Serve immediately or refrigerate until ready to serve. Toss again before serving if dressing has settled.
