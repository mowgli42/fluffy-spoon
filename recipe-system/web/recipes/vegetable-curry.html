Creamy Vegetable Curry

A fragrant and creamy vegetable curry made with coconut milk, aromatic spices, and fresh vegetables. This vegetarian dish is flavorful, satisfying, and naturally gluten-free.

Ingredients

Preparation Steps

1
Heat coconut oil in a large pot over medium heat.
2
Add diced onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
3
Stir in minced garlic and ginger, cook for 30 seconds until fragrant.
4
Add curry powder and turmeric, stir well to coat vegetables, cook for 1 minute.
5
Add cauliflower, potatoes, and carrots. Stir to combine with the spice mixture.
6
Pour in coconut milk and vegetable broth. Bring to a simmer.
7
Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer uncovered for 20-25 minutes until vegetables are tender.
8
Stir in lime juice and season with salt to taste.
9
Serve hot over rice, garnished with fresh cilantro.
